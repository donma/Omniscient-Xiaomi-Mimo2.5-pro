# T011 Dental Clinic Template Generation Prompt

## Template Overview
Create a professional dental clinic website with a clean, medical aesthetic that builds trust with patients.

## Design Direction
- **Style**: Ultra-clean medical, trust-building, professional
- **Color Palette**: Light blue (#e8f4fd), White (#ffffff), Medical blue (#2196F3), Green accent (#4caf50)
- **Typography**: Inter for English headings, Noto Sans TC for Chinese text
- **Layout**: Clean grid-based sections with generous whitespace

## Hero Section
Split layout with smiling dentist image on right, text left with trust badges (years of experience, patient count, certifications).

## Required Sections (Homepage)
1. Hero with trust badges
2. Services Grid (implants, whitening, orthodontics, general checkup, etc.)
3. Doctor Profile with credentials
4. Technology & Equipment showcase
5. Patient Before/After gallery
6. Insurance & FAQ preview
7. Appointment CTA
8. Location Map with contact info

## Card Design
White cards with blue accent borders, clean line icons, subtle shadows.

## Navigation
White background, blue active states, sticky header with clinic phone number.

## Special Requirements
- LocalBusiness Schema markup for dental practice
- Insurance provider logos section
- Before/after image comparison
- Appointment booking form
- Emergency contact prominently displayed
- All images from Unsplash dental/medical collections
